Lodging & Grounds

A lodge, shared meals, and room to gather

The property includes overnight lodging for up to 32 guests, dining space, a meeting hall, game room, separate dormitory areas, bathrooms, fields, trails, and mountain views.

What groups can use

The lodge is the center of the property, with space for meals, sessions, games, rest, and informal conversation. Outdoor areas can support recreation, walks, campfires, and quiet time.
